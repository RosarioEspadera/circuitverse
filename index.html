📚 Lessons

Lesson 1: Voltage, Current, Resistance

Understand the three basics of circuits and Ohm’s Law.

  • Voltage = electrical potential difference (V)
  • Current = flow of charge (A)
  • Resistance = opposition to current (Ω)
  • Ohm’s Law: V = I × R

Lesson 2: Circuit Components

Resistors, capacitors, inductors, diodes, transistors – the building blocks of electronics.
